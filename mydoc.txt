What is SeamlessHR? 
A platform used to maximize people’s end-to-end HR technology for hiring, training, and managing staff. It performs the core HR functions for a company, rather seamlessly. 
Some of the biggest companies around the world use seamless HR (MTN, FCMB, STERLING, AXA)
